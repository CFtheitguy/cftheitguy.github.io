Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about onboarding, support, pricing, and security.

What does onboarding look like?

We start with a discovery session and a light audit of endpoints, network, and cloud. Then we deploy agents, document your environment, define SLAs, and roll out quick wins in the first 14–30 days.

How do you price Managed Services?

Most clients choose a per‑device or per‑user monthly plan covering monitoring, patching, security, and support. Project work (migrations, new sites, etc.) is scoped separately with a fixed or hourly quote.

Can you support remote and multi‑site teams?

Yes. We manage VPNs, Zero Trust access, device compliance via Intune, and provide remote helpdesk across time zones. We also standardize Wi‑Fi and switching for consistent performance in each location.

What security controls do you implement?

MFA/conditional access, least‑privilege accounts, next‑gen AV/EDR, email filtering, patch baselines, incident response runbooks, and tested backups. We align to practical CIS controls for SMEs.

Do you work with existing IT teams?

Absolutely. We can be your full MSP or an extension to handle helpdesk, patching, or projects while your team focuses on strategy.

How fast can we start?

Discovery can begin this week; agent deployment usually the same or next day. Full onboarding typically completes within 2–4 weeks depending on scope.

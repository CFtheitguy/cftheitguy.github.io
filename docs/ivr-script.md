# IVR Greeting Script

## Main Greeting
Thank you for calling Linear Tech. We’re glad you reached us.

If you know your party’s extension, you may dial it at any time.

For Technical Support, press 1.
For Sales and New Services, press 2.
For Billing and Accounts, press 3.
To repeat these options, press 9.
To speak with the next available team member, stay on the line.

## After-Hours Greeting
Thank you for calling Linear Tech. Our office is currently closed.

If this is a critical issue affecting business operations, press 1 to leave an urgent support message.
For all other requests, please leave a message with your name, company, and callback number, and we will return your call as soon as possible on the next business day.

You can also email us anytime at support@linearit.co.
